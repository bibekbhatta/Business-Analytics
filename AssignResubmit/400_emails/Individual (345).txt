description(pour la version française, voir après le texte en anglais) art’s planet – edition 2017 crowdfunding climate, migration and solidarity: the art of collectiveness an international artistic and citizen call to action on the road toward the 2017 international climate conference in bonn the objective of the project is to engage citizens and artists worldwide through an interactive art exhibition, supported by the production of an advocacy report along with an art book, and to support concrete projects bringing help to climate migrants and vulnerable communities. everyone is invited to be part of an international art and citizen initiative under art’s planet by energies 2050 . the exhibition and reports that come along will be presented during the next international climate change conference (23rd conference of the parties of the united nations framework convention on climate change, or cop23) held in bonn, germany, from the 6th to the 17th of november 2017. for the 2017 edition of art’s planet, the theme is: “climate, migration and solidarity: the art of collectiveness” conditions for participation and documentation are available at: http://artsplanet2017.energies2050.org?lang=en the objective is first to offer citizens, and especially young people and the art community, the opportunity to freely express themselves and participate in their own way to the major challenges our societies are facing. secondly, art’s planet 2017 edition aims to strengthen its impact by making a direct contribution to concrete sustainability projects. to that extent, an international auction for artworks offered by artists will be organized at the end of cop23 to raise funds for climate actions. presentation of the initiative and partners: http://energies2050.org/wp content/uploads/2017/07/2017 07 05 leaflet_arts planet_edition 2017_en.pdf each participant will be invited to make an artistic performance expressing its perception of climate change and migration. all contributors will be encouraged to use when possible recycled materials and to make the project a collective and collaborative one. the objective will then be: to capture the performance with high quality pictures that will be exposed during cop23 to send the artwork to bonn (for those who want) to display the artworks for a local exhibition (for those who decide not to send the artwork to bonn). modalities of participation: http://energies2050.org/wp content/uploads/2017/07/2017 07 05 rules_arts planet_edition 2017_en.pdf everyone is more than welcome and there is no restriction for participating to the exhibition. involved participants can be cities, local authorities, neighbourhoods, groups, artists or communities of artists, citizens… each will have the opportunity, with its own performance, to take part to this global call to action. get involved or ask for more information: email address: artsplanet2017@energies2050.org webpage: http://artsplanet2017.energies2050.org * partners of art’s planet 2017 edition at this time, the partners of art’s planet 2017 edition are (non exhaustive list) energies 2050, yael arts world, the institut de la francophonie pour le développement durable (ifdd   subsidiary body of the organisation internationale de la francophonie gathering 84 countries), united cities and local governments of africa (uclg africa – gathering all african cities), environmental art collective foundation (enacof), one environment, afrique environnement plus, the network of communicators for the environment and information in central africa (receiac)… energies 2050 is a non profit, project based organisation operating without public funding. all partners of art’s planet 2017 edition are non financial partners. our common objective is to federate citizens across the world and to invite additional partners to join the initiative. this being said, the running costs of the initiative and its associated activities remain a major constraint for its success. within the art’s planet 2017 edition, we want to: produce an advocacy report to be published during cop23 print high quality large size pictures of artworks received to be exposed during cop23 realize a art/advocacy exhibition during cop23, which implies substantive costs (mainly to rent a space where to organize the exhibition) realize and publish an art book after cop23 organize an auction for offered artworks to support concrete projects to help climate migrants and vulnerable communities energies 2050 is a well recognized non governmental organisation. gathering members and partners of more than sixty nationalities, energies 2050 works internationally to set up a new, positive and inclusive development model. as a collective adventure in the quest for better ways of living together, energies 2050 is committed to the great transition, including the energy transition, sustainable cities and regions and the shift towards a more humane, plural and united society, bringing peace and respecting the common goods of humanity. the momentum is of the utmost importance… we need to act and to federate to change the trends of our development trajectories. this action is part of our global strategy to mobilize women and men of good will to tackle challenges and to transform constraints into opportunities. to make this initiative a success, energies 2050 already pre booked a central space of 50m² in the bonn climate change conference from the 6th till the 17th of november 2017. more than 100 000 visitors are expected to attend the bonn conference. to make this initiative possible, we need your help. feel concerned, testify and act we are all actors of change ! back art’s planet   edition 2017             art’s planet – crowdfunding édition 2017 climat, migration et solidarité : l’art du collectif un appel à l’action artistique et citoyenne vers la conférence internationale sur le climat de bonn 2017 l'objectif de l'édition 2017 d’art’s planet est d'impliquer les citoyens et artistes du monde entier à travers une exposition d’art interactive, supportée par la production d’un document de plaidoyer ainsi qu’un livre d’art, mais aussi de supporter des projets concrets en faveur de migrants climatiques et des communautés vulnérables. chacun et chacune est invité(e) à prendre part à une exposition artistique internationale dans le cadre de l’initiative art’s planet by energies 2050 . l’exposition et les documents associés seront présentés lors de la prochaine conférence internationale sur le climat (la 23ème conférence des parties à la convention cadre des nations unies sur les changements climatiques, ou cop23) qui se tiendra à bonn en allemagne du 6 au 17 novembre 2017. pour l'édition 2017 de art’s planet, le thème est le suivant : « climat, migration et solidarité : l'art du collectif ». les conditions de participation et autres documents utiles sont disponibles à : http://artsplanet2017.energies2050.org l'objectif de cette initiative internationale artistique et citoyenne est tout d’abord d'offrir aux citoyens, et surtout aux jeunes et à la communauté artistique, l'opportunité de s'exprimer librement et de participer à leur manière aux défis majeurs auxquels nos sociétés sont confrontées. d’autre part, l’édition 2017 d’art’s planet vise à renforcer son impact en faisant en sorte que l’initiative puisse contribuer directement à des projets concrets de solidarité et de durabilité. dans cette optique, une enchère internationale pour les œuvres reçues et offertes par les artistes sera mise en place après l'exposition, à la fin de la cop23, afin d’obtenir un financement d’actions climatiques. présentation de l’initiative et des partenaires : http://energies2050.org/wp content/uploads/2017/07/2017 07 05 leaflet_arts planet_edition 2017_fr.pdf chaque participant devra faire une performance artistique en lien avec sa perception du changement climatique et des problèmes de migration connexes. tous les participants seront encouragés à utiliser le cas échéant des matériaux de récupération et à faire en sorte que leur projet soit collaboratif. l'objectif sera soit de : photographier la performance artistique afin d’exposer les photos (haute qualité) lors de la cop23 envoyer directement l'œuvre à bonn (pour ceux qui le désirent) conserver l'installation dans des locaux dédiés pour une exposition locale (pour ceux qui décideraient de ne pas envoyer leurs travaux à bonn) modalités de participation : http://energies2050.org/wp content/uploads/2017/07/2017 07 05 reglement_arts planet_edition 2017_fr.pdf toute contribution est la bienvenue et il n’y a aucune restriction pour participer à l'exposition. les participants peuvent être des villes, des autorités locales, des quartiers, des groupes de personnes, des artistes ou des communautés d'artistes, des individus... chacun aura l'occasion avec sa propre performance de participer à une action mondiale. pour vous impliquer ou en savoir plus : adresse email : artsplanet2017@energies2050.org page internet : http://artsplanet2017.energies2050.org * partenaires d’art’s planet edition 2017 : a ce jour, les partenaires de l’édition 2017 d’art’s planet sont (liste non exhaustive) : energies 2050, yael arts
